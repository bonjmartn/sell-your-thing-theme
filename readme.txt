FREE AND PREMIUM VERSIONS
------------------------------

This theme comes in two versions: Free and Premium.

Free Features

- Sell your product by customizing your homepage with the WordPress Customizer
- Large photo overlaid with a hero image, headline, text, and button
- FontAwesome icons for your product features
- Call to Action bar to drive conversions
- Screenshots and reviews of your product
- Responsive design
- A social media widget for the footer
- Detailed documentation

Premium Features

The Premium version has all of the free features, plus:

- Choose your own color for links
- Choose your own color for icons
- 14 Google Fonts
- Full width, left sidebar, and right sidebar Page layouts
- Different sidebars for Posts and Pages
- 20 photos for your homepage, selected specifically for this theme and already cropped to the ideal size
- End of Posts widget area
- Professional tech support
